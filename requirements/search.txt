Title: Browse and search for available credit cards

As a potential customer
I want to search and explore credit cards on amex.com
So that I can find a card that suits my needs

Acceptance Criteria:
User can navigate to the credit cards section of amex.com
User can view a list of available credit cards
User can use filters (e.g., rewards, travel, cashback) to refine results
User can click on a credit card to view details (benefits, fees, rewards)
User can see clear card comparison or highlights without logging in
User receives relevant results based on selected filters or search criteria
